Automation Transformation Proposal

A full build to automate Acme's core operational workflows, from intake to fulfillment, with a human approval layer on every step that carries risk. Designed to remove repetitive work, protect quality, and scale without adding headcount.

The opportunity

Acme runs on manual effort that does not need to be manual.

Today your team moves data by hand across disconnected systems, re-enters the same information many times, and relies on a few key people to keep the back office running. That is slow, it is fragile, and it does not scale. This project rebuilds those flows as one connected system that runs on its own and surfaces only the decisions that truly need a person.

What we build

Four phases, each shipped and working before the next begins.

Phase 1

Discovery and architecture

We map every workflow, document each system and its data, and design the single source of truth that the whole platform will run on. You approve the blueprint before any build starts.

Phase 2

Core build

We build the central database, the automation engine, and the connectors to your existing tools. Repetitive data entry and routing become automatic, with full logging on every action.

Phase 3

Integration and rollout

We connect the system to the rest of your stack, add the human approval checkpoints for any step with legal or financial risk, and roll it out to your team with training and live support.

Phase 4

Optimization and handoff

We tune performance against real usage, harden the system, and hand over clean documentation so your team owns it with confidence. Ongoing support is available after handoff.
